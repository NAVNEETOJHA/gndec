The Finite Element Method (FEM) is a numerical and computer based technique of solving a variety of practical engineering problems that can arise in different fields. It is recognised by developers and users as one of the most powerful numerical analysis tolls ever adevised to analyse complex promblems of engineering. FEM formulation will be elobrated for various practical engineering problems related to civil engineer, mechanical engineer and electrical engineer involving 1D, 2D and 3D problems of structural engineering, fluid mechanics, heat transfer etc.
