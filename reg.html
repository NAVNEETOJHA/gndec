There is no registration fees for the participants from AICTE recognized Institutes. However, at par cheque of Rs 1,000/- (drawn in favour of "Director, Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College, Ludhiana", payable at Ludhiana) should be enclosed with the application form which will be refunded to the participants who will attend the course. The other participants will have to pay registration fee of Rs. 5000/-.
